About

Our Mission

Our mission is to create balanced meals that are affordable and convenient. OneYum will leverage innovative processes like composting and recycling to minimize waste whenever possible. Create an infrastructure of food production that sustains and meets the needs of every community and their existing infrastructures.

Our Vision

Our vision is to challenge the way that food works. We will consistently focus on nurturing the growth of local food production through civic initiatives, buying power, and innovative repurposing practices that redefine the ‘food-to-table’ process to better meet the evolving needs in every community, drive sustainable environmental practices, and reward everyone involved with more opportunities to better themselves.
